Corporate Profile

"Büyüknisan Law Firm is an Istanbul-based legal practice committed to providing strategic and result-oriented legal counsel within the framework of universal legal principles. By synthesising academic depth with extensive practical experience, particularly in the dynamic fields of Immigration & Nationality Law and Healthcare Law, we facilitate a transparent and prestigious representation process tailored to the specific legal requirements of each client."

Fields of Expertise

Immigration & Nationality Law

Management of naturalisation proceedings, residence and work permit applications, and deportation disputes.

Healthcare & Medical Law

Strategic counsel regarding clinical negligence (malpractice) claims and the protection of patient rights.

Tort Law & Damages

Judicial management of material and non-pecuniary compensation claims arising from tortious liability and accidents.

Criminal Defence & Advocacy

Professional legal representation throughout investigation and trial phases within the scope of fair trial principles.

Administrative Litigation

Annulment of administrative acts and the management of full remedy actions before Judicial Authorities.

Execution & Bankruptcy Law

Professional management of debt collection, enforcement of court orders, and insolvency proceedings.
